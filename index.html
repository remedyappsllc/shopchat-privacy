ShopChat AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ShopChat AI collects the following information when installed on a Wix store: store product catalog data, shipping rules, business name and location, and store owner contact email. This information is used solely to power the AI chat assistant.

2. How We Use Your Information

Store data is used to generate accurate responses to customer questions via the ShopChat AI chat widget. We do not sell, share, or distribute your store data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Store data is cached temporarily in memory on our servers to enable real-time chat responses. Data is not permanently stored in a database and is cleared when the app is uninstalled.

4. Third-Party Services

ShopChat AI uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate chat responses. Customer chat messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. We do not store individual customer chat conversations.

5. Customer Data

Visitor chat messages are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently. No personally identifiable information from store visitors is collected or retained.

6. Data Security

All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS. API keys and credentials are stored securely as environment variables and are never exposed publicly.

7. Your Rights

Store owners may request deletion of their store data at any time by uninstalling the app or contacting us directly.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: support@remedyappsllc.com
